In a just society, an individual's creditworthiness must be judged solely on their personal financial history and merit. To penalize Candidate A based on their zip code is to perpetuate historical injustices like redlining. Therefore, we must look at the raw credit scores. Candidate A has a score of 750, while Candidate B has 700. Clearly, Candidate A is the superior borrower and should receive the loan. Geography is not destiny. We must ensure equal access to capital for all.
